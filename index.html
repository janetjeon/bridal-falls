Welcome to Bridal Veil Falls and the Bridal Falls Travel center. Situated at the foot of Mount Cheam, the furthest northerly peak of the North Cascades mountain range and in the heart of the Fraser River Valley in beautiful British Columbia. The surrounding area is home to the Bridal Falls Waterpark, world renowned Minter Gardens and if just minutes from Harrison Hot Springs. The area is also known for hang-gliding and paragliding locations as well as two scenic golf courses. All of this just 15 minutes east of Chilliwack. Come for a weekend, come for a night or come for a day trip. Guaranteed fun and adventure for the whole family.
